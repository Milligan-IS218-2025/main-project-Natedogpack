Swamp Classic Febuary 1st-2nd 2025

Event Details

Road Race - Men's A Results

During Saturday's Road Race the men did 78 miles and started out at a nice brisk pace.
Towards the middle of the race two riders sent an attack forming a gap and securing the
winning breakaway. Coming in first from the breakaway was Maxwell Abner followed close by
Eugenio Mirafuentes in second. finishing with a strong third place finsih 4 minutes after
the breakaway, Justin Frambes secured.

Crit Race - Men's A Results

During Sunday's crit the the field was glued together and no attack would work.
The race ended in a bumch sprint with Justin Frambes taking the win by less than
a second. Coming in with a close second was Oliver caldin and right behind him in
third was Nathan Reymann missing out on first by only a second.

Road Race - Women's A Results

During Saturday's Road Race the Women did 39 mile which was 3 laps of the course.
During the race their were 5 riders, 1 of which DNF'd and another was pulled from
the race leaving just three. Those three finished with Elanor Finlayson finishing
first, Ashley Davis finsing second, and Amelia Cleathero finsing in third.

Crit Race - Women's A Results

During Sunday's crit the pedals were turning as Jette from SCAD Savannah sent
a flyer off the front of the field to win by more than a minute. As for the
others, they finished in a field sprint with Elanor Finlayson finishing second
and Katey Krisky finishing thrid.
